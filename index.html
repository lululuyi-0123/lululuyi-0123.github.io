H!PONG

Douyin Mini Program

This mini program is a location-based, AI-recommended tool for organizing offline gatherings and team-building activities. It helps users quickly determine a meeting spot, generate activity plans, and initiate group events.

Four functional modules

City Meet-up

Supports simultaneous multi-user location tracking and uses AI to recommend meeting spots based on location and individual preferences.

Team-building Plan Creation

Input the duration, type, budget, and more, and the AI will help generate a detailed, customizable team-building plan.

Social Gathering

Users can create or join group events, and the system intelligently recommends sessions they may be interested in based on their preferences.

MeowTrack

WeChat Mini Program

Designed for cat lovers in a specific area, this “cat-petting map” guides users to friendly cats, supports tracking stray cats’ whereabouts, and promotes healthy feeding practices.

Four functional modules

Sharing Community

All cat-related posts you’ve published can be viewed here, where you can see their adorable moments.

Cat Profiles

All registered cats are listed here, where you can view their activity trails and feeding records.

Add to collection

After you favorite a cat, it will appear on your profile for easy viewing.

Post

Posts you publish will appear in the community feed. You can upload cute photos of the cats, your current location, and what you’ve fed them.
